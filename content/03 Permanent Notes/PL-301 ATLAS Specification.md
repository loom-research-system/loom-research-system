# ATLAS Specification — Institutional Landscape Mapping

## Purpose

ATLAS maps the institutional landscape of a governance domain: the actors, authorities, funding flows, and decision-making relationships that constitute the governance architecture.

## Status

This specification is in Draft. The methodology has been demonstrated in Cases [[PL-402|#001]], [[PL-403|#002]], and [[PL-404|#003]] through Stage 2 of the [[PL-201 Loom Analysis Protocol|Analysis Protocol]] (Governance Architecture Mapping). The standalone specification will formalize:

- Inputs required for institutional mapping
- Node types (agencies, departments, contractors, courts, legislatures, etc.)
- Relationship types (authority, funding, oversight, dependency, coordination)
- Mapping conventions and visualization standards
- Outputs (actor inventories, authority matrices, network diagrams)

## Development Path

Full specification to be developed during Phase 2 (Systems Development).
