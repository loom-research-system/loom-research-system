# Evidence Standards

## Evidence Levels

| Level | Standard | Example |
|-------|----------|---------|
| A | Direct documentary evidence | Statute, regulation, court opinion, official government report |
| B | Government data or independent empirical research | Census data, peer-reviewed quantitative study |
| C | Credible secondary analysis | Academic literature, GAO report, investigative journalism |
| D | Inference supported by multiple independent sources | Pattern identified across sources without direct documentary confirmation |
| E | Hypothesis requiring further validation | Plausible explanation not yet supported by sufficient evidence |

## Purpose

Evidence standards serve three functions in Project Loom:

1. **Transparency** — Readers can assess the strength of claims independently.
2. **Consistency** — Different analysts applying the framework should reach comparable assessments.
3. **Humility** — The framework distinguishes between what is well-established and what is hypothesized.
